Can you create the perfect date before it's too late?



I. THE BEGINNING.

After an exhausting day of work, you head to the local tavern to relax. A beautiful creature approaches you and offers to purchase a drink for you. After blinking away confusion, you realize the creature speaking to you. It's...

The Big Bad Wolf
The Beast
Aquila the Eagle
Cerberus the Three-Headed Dog

II. GREETINGS.

After agreeing to see each other again soon, you mark the date in your calendar. Soon, the day arrives, and you're wondering what flowers to get for him. You decide on a bouquet of...

White lilies
Red roses
Blue roses
Forget-me-not

III. LOCATION.

The journey there is difficult, and you're sweating by the time you get there. Still, your eyes widen at the meeting place. It completely embodies his personality, at least from what little you know if him. You've arrived at...

A snug cottage deep in the forest
A castle surrounded by greenery and rivers
The heavens, filled with silver clouds and gittering stars
A cozy den tucked safely under the earth

IV. AMBIANCE.

When you arrive, he greets you warmly with a glint in his eyes. He places the flowers in a vase and leads you around his place, all the while with his claws at your back. Despite this gentleman being not-quite-human, you still marvel at his taste in beautiful decor:

Garlands of flowers and herbs on the walls, with candles and cushions on the floor
An unravelling staircase in the middle of the room, with grand paintings and towering bookshelves along the walls
Jars of stardust and the scent of rain in the air
A roaring fireplace with old, cushioned chairs and a plush rug in front of it
